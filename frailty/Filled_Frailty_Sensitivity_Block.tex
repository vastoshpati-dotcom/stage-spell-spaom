\subsection{Process-Frailty Sensitivity Analysis}

A secondary random-intercept extension was fitted:
\[
\log\mu_{iar}=\log\eta_a+u_i-\beta_s\log s_{ir},
\qquad
u_i\sim\mathcal N(0,\sigma_u^2).
\]

The fixed-effects estimate was \(\widehat{\beta}_s=0.5198\), whereas the frailty estimate was \(0.4818\).
The estimated frailty standard deviation was \(0.1659\).
The process-bootstrap interval for the frailty-adjusted spell effect was \([0.3737,\,0.5555]\).
The parametric-bootstrap likelihood-ratio test of \(\sigma_u^2=0\) gave \(p=0.0601\).

\textit{Sensitivity interpretation:} The stage-spell effect remains positive after the frailty extension, but evidence for additional random-intercept heterogeneity is limited.
